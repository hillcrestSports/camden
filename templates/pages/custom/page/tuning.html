BELT WAX

Fast and Easy

It only takes 30 seconds and we can squeeze one in anytime. Just drop by and get a quick wax on the way to the mountain. It's not a substitute for an Iron Wax, but it's an easy way to keep you sliding for a while.

The heated, wax infused roller and buff wheel lay a thin layer on your base and then buff it off for a smooth finish.

IRON WAX

Good things take time

Nothing beats a good wax on a blue-bird powder day. We use a temperature appropriate wax and iron it in by hand. Then scrape and buff for the perfect finish.

Our techs take the time to make sure it's done right. We clean your base, apply the correct wax, iron it in at a specific temperature for that base and wax type and then let it cool. After cooling we scrape the excess wax by hand and then use a series of brushes to expose the structure of the base material for the best possible hydrophobic effect.

EDGE TUNING

For that extra edge

Your edges are like the tires on your car. They're what hold you to the snow when you turn. getting your baord/skis edged is like replacing your tires and getting an alignment. We sharpen and bevel every inch to a specific standard.

Our steady-handed technicians have decades of experience so when you drop off you skis/board you can be confident that it's coming back to you with the best edges you can get on a machine.

HAND EDGE TUNING

Smooth is fast

Instead of a machine we use the well trained touch of a certified technicians hand. Depending on the hardness of the steel used in your edges, we make multiple passes with different grit stones at a user specified degree.

We're able to change degree and sharpness along the length of your edge for different snow conditions and racing styles. It's not cheap, but its worth it when hundredths of a second count.

BASE REPAIR

Patch the holes

We all want to get on the hill as early in the season as possible and stay as late as possible. This usually means riding on sub-par snow conditions and that leads to base damage.

From scratches to core-shots, we can fix them all. Base repairs require a Full Tune, extra time and a little extra cash to bring your base back to factory condition, but come on, who doesn't want to get another couple of seasons out of their favorite board/skis?

BASE GRIND

Like new again

It's green, makes a terrible noise, throws sparks and will take your arm off if you're not careful. No, it's not a dragon. It's our Wintersteiger base tuning machine.

When a wax just isn't enough to keep those little dings and scratches at bay any longer, it's time for a Base Grind. A spinning cylinder of magical materials grinds the base fractions of millimeters each pass while re-adding water shedding structure for optimum performance.
