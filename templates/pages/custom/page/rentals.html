Ski Package Rental

To have an enjoyable skiing experience, it’s important to have a pair of skis that are the perfect length for you. To do this, we at Hillcrest Sports will measure your height and weight, learn your skill level as well as learn what terrain you’ll be on. The type of skis we’ll give you will be based on the type of skiing you plan to do.
All-Mountain skis are great for a mix of terrain including groomed and powder. They are suited for all skill levels and age groups.
All-Mountain wide skies work well in groomed areas and powder areas. They are slightly wider in the waist than the all-mountain ski. Wide skis are only available for demo.
Powder skis are best if you plan on skiing in deep powder snow.

Rental Ski Boots

Your ski boots are perhaps the most important part of your rental package. If they’re too stiff, too small or too large, you will not have a great experience. This is why we at Hillcrest Sports always have you try on the boot first and if it’s not comfortable, we’ll try other sizes and models until we find you a perfect match. Everyone has different feet sizes and shapes as well as skill levels when it comes to skiing and all should be taken into consideration when selecting a boot.

Rental Ski Poles

Your ski poles help you balance and time your turns. Whether you’ve forgotten, misplaced, or broke your own ski poles, we at Hillcrest provide adult and children’s ski poles for rent.

*Youth pricing is for skis 140cm or shorter, and for size 6 boot or under.

Kayak Rentals

Kayaking is a watersport that allows you to enjoy nature from a different perspective. It can be a calm and relaxing experience set on a mellow lake or a fun and exciting adventure on the rapids of your local river. You decide. The possibilities for kayaking are endless. If you’re thinking about kayaking, Hillcrest Sports offers daily and seasonal kayak rentals. We carry a full range of kayaks including single kayaks and tandem kayaks-- so you can explore alone or with a friend! Our kayak rentals start at the low price of $ 40 and come with everything you need to have a safe kayaking experience including your paddles and your personal flotation device (PFD). We also offer recreational passes in-store.

Where to Kayak

There are several popular kayaking spots in the Portland OR and Vancouver WA area. Transport Kayaks

If you’re thinking about renting a kayak, you’ll want to consider how you’re going to transport it. There are a variety of ways to safely transport a kayak. Kayaks can be tied down to your vehicle with cam straps and water-resistant rope. You can also invest in a roof rack or smaller trailer to making transporting your kayak effortless.

Wake Sports Rentals

Wakeboards and Wakesurf Boards, while previously belived to be one singular type of object. It had been revieled by Ryan that they are in fact two seperate things. One is a different shape than the other. Wakeboarding is a water sport in which the rider, standing on a wakeboard (a short board with foot bindings), is towed behind a motorboat across its wake and especially up off the crest in order to perform aerial maneuvers. Wakesurfing is a water sport in which a rider trails behind a boat, riding the boat's wake without being directly pulled by the boat.

Mountaineering Package Rentals

Crampons: These work with your boots and have sharp metal spikes on the bottom that help you sink your feet into the ice. Crampons are built in a variety of materials dependent upon the activity. Hillcrest rents semi-rigid, steel crampons in both universal straps and semi automatic straps that will fit a wide range of sole types. Hillcrest has a brand new fleet of the CAMP Stalker Univeral and CAMP Stalker Semi-Auto.

Ice Axe: Use an ice axe to break through the ice and keep your balance as well as to self arrest during a sudden fall. Ice axes are sized based on intended activity and height of user. Hillcrest currently stocks the CAMP Neve in a wide variety of sizes.

Helmet: Protect yourself against loose, falling rocks and ice with a helmet. All Mountaineering helmets are certified for overhead protection unlike most ski and snowboard helmets which are certified for side-protection. We offer the CAMP Titan helmets. Though you can buy mountaineering equipment, you can save some money when you rent gear instead. Hillcrest has all the mountaineering equipment you need that you can rent for 48 hours. To reserve the equipment, stop by our store!
